Headline Activities

Asafo Companies

The unique character of the Aboakyer festival can be attributed to the development of the Asafo groups into an institution purposefully for providing the King with a live deer annually to propitiate Penkye Otu.

Preparation for the Festival

Active preparation towards the festival starts soon after the Easter holidays. Before that, the youth begin to assemble on canoes on the sea shore to learn popular asafo songs.

The hunt and the climax

By 4.00 am on the hunting day, Saturday, asafo members are awakened by the sounding of drums, bugle, rattles and bells to start trooping to their respective meeting places.
